Uncluttered minimal interface with quick function access.

* Type a few characters and tap enter to find apps.
* Suggestions adapt to use.
* Your data never leaves your device.
* Small (under 250 KB).
* Save battery.

Similar to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spotlight_(software)">Spotlight</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_(software)">Alfred</a>, the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windows_Search">Windows search bar</a> or GNOME's <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNOME_Shell">Dash menu</a> .
